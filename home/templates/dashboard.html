Dashboard

Welcome to PoraPlan.pro dashboard! The main goal of PoraPlan.pro is to create and hold the meetings.
The shortest path to create your first meetings is do the following steps:

  1. Create project by pressing "Add new project" button;
  2. Fulfill all the important information and press "Save";
  3. Go to meetings and press "Add new meetings" button;
  4. Go through all the steps and press "Submit";
  5. Now, you've done! Have a good meetings!

Projects

To create project go to Projects section. This is what you should do first!

Each project includes the following information: Project name, Description, Deadline, Status and Team members.

Meetings

To create meetings go to Meetings section. After creating first project you can assign as much meetings you need.
Any meeting has 3 main parameters: related Project, Meeting type (for additional information and sorting) and
related Meeting (to create a series of them). Here are the hints for each step of creating process.

General tab
Here you need to specify meeting Name, Date, Purpose and key Subject of the meeting.
Agenda tab
Here you attach the document describing what the subjects you will go through. Here is a good example.
Materials tab
Here you can attach any additional handouts, documents and forms that your participants will need for.
Time&Place tab
Here you can chose the available rooms near your location (detection is done automatically).
Set minimum requirements for the convenient meeting, see if the rooms available, select and press "Submit".

Account

To update the account go to Account section.

In this section you can update your profile, email, passrod and other important information regarding to you.
